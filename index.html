Claudia Palacios

Biology Undergraduate Student - claudia.palacios@upch.pe

Hello! A determined, proactive and resilient Biology student with an interest in the area of Psychoneuroimmunology and Neurobiology, in how the mood in conjunction with other factors affects an individual's health. Research assistant at the UPCH Laboratory of Bioinformatics and Molecular Biology for the development of methods to diagnose autism in children. I am passionate about participating in initiatives that promote scientific and technological dissemination. Current Coordinator of Neurosciences at Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society UPCH.
